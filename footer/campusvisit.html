HOW TO REACH US ?

Lovely Professional University is easily accessible as it is located on the Jalandhar-Delhi G.T. Road (Punjab) and is well connected by rail and road; having a reasonable distance from the airports, bus stands, railway stations etc.






UNIVERSITY ACCOMODATION

Hotel

Our accommodations are available year-round through the Office of Residential Services. It includes limited number of guest rooms for the visiting guests.

AC Apartment

The University has a limited number of rooms for visitors staying for short periods of time. Visitors range from guests of students to new members of staff and visiting academics.

Hostel
