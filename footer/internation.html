International Admissions 2017-18


Now take LPUNEST in your own Country
LPUNEST test centres are now in 50 countries as well as 140 cities of India

LPUNEST is National Entrance and Scholarship Test for the admission to various programmes in Lovely Professional University. This test forms the basis of eligibility for some of the programmes and scholarship for most of the programmes. Online Registration for LPUNEST-2017 (B.Tech, MBA, Law, Hotel Management, Agriculture and Design Programmes) will begin from 1st October 2016.

Scholarship is offered on the basis of performance in LPUNEST in all the programmes of the University. Details of LPUNEST-2017 for other programmes will be released in due course.


Registrations for LPUNEST-2017 will begin from 1st Week of November 2016 onwards. The Test will be conducted in online mode (Pen and Paper Mode test is not allowed) as per the schedule

This test will have twofold advantage as it will act as Eligibility Test as well as Scholarship Test.

Eligibility Test:  LPUNEST-2017 will provide a gateway to all engineering programmes in LPU and will act as Entrance test for admission in Engineering Programmes at LPU.

Scholarship: LPU envisages to usher an era of accessible and affordable quality higher education to the youth of our country, as on the basis of LPUNEST-2017 score, the university intends to award extensive Scholarship upto 7.5 lac per student. For details of scholarship please refer “Fee and Scholarship”.
