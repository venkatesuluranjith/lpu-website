Scholarship on the basis of LPUNEST

Lovely Professional University strongly believes that monetary constraints should not be an obstacle for a student to have access to quality education. So, the university extends Scholarship Scheme to students on various parameters. More than 30000 students have been awarded with scholarships worth Rs.300+ crores in last 5 years.


Following are the details of criteria and scholarship amount applicable
